As a library administrator, you have full control over book management and user activities.

What You Can Do:

✅ Create New Books – Expand the library’s collection with new titles.

✅ Update Book Information – Modify details such as title, author, or availability.

✅ Delete Books – Remove books from the system.

✅ Manage User Loans – Track borrowed books, update due dates, and confirm returns.

✅ Monitor Users – View users’ book reservations and overdue returns.
